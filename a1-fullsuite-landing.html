Built-in AI · Across the entire workflow

AI that scales your catalog ops without scaling headcount.

Smart category mapping

Our embedding + LLM pipeline maps every product to the right marketplace category — even SKUs your team has never listed before. Stop maintaining taxonomy spreadsheets across thousands of products.

7 to 14 days from contract signing to fully live, depending on data complexity. Your onboarding manager runs daily check-ins for the first week and weekly check-ins for the first month. We’ve never had a migration take longer than 30 days.

We have native integrations with QuickBooks and NetSuite. Custom ERP, WMS, and supplier-feed integrations are handled by our engineering team — typically 1–3 weeks of build time. We’ve integrated with SAP, Acumatica, ShipStation, ShipHero, 3PL Central, and dozens of custom systems.
