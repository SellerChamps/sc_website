Everything you need to know

Switching is easier than you think

Common questions from sellers considering the move from SellerCloud, Zentail, Rithum, or SureDone.

How long does it take to switch from SellerCloud, Zentail, or Rithum?+

Most sellers are fully up and running within 2–5 business days. Unlike SellerCloud or Rithum which require weeks of onboarding and professional services, SellerChamp is designed to get you live fast. Our team handles the heavy lifting — you just point us to your existing catalog and we take it from there.

Will I lose my listings or inventory data when I migrate?+

No. We migrate your existing listings, inventory quantities, pricing rules, and templates. Your live listings on Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and Shopify stay active throughout the migration — there’s no downtime and no gap in selling. We run a parallel sync until you’re confident everything is correct before cutting over.

Is SellerChamp really a full-suite platform, not just a listing tool?+

Yes — SellerChamp covers the complete multichannel commerce stack: bulk listing, real-time inventory sync, order management, automated repricing, dropshipping/supplier automation, FBA + 3PL fulfillment routing, kitting & bundling, purchase orders, profit reporting, and discounted shipping labels. It’s the same feature set as SellerCloud or Zentail — just without the six-figure annual contract.

How does SellerChamp compare to ProductsUp or SureDone?+

ProductsUp is purely a product feed management tool — it does not do inventory sync, order management, or fulfillment automation. It’s a feed optimizer, not an MCM platform. SureDone is closer to SellerChamp in scope but starts at $500–$2,000+/month and lacks SellerChamp’s AI listing content, bulk lister speed, and the quality of live support. If you need a complete operational platform with fast onboarding, SellerChamp wins on all three counts.

Do you require an annual contract or cancellation fee?+

None. SellerChamp is month-to-month — cancel any time with no penalty. Compare that to SellerCloud and Rithum which lock you into 12-month contracts with hefty exit clauses. We earn your business every single month.

How does SellerChamp pricing work?+

Pricing is based on what you actually need — SKU count, order volume, channels, and features. Plans start at $399/month with no setup fees, no per-channel fees, no GMV%, and no surprise add-ons. Get a custom quote in 60 seconds — no commitment or credit card required.

What channels does SellerChamp support?+

SellerChamp supports Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Shopify, Etsy, TikTok Shop, Shein, Temu, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Google Shopping, and more — with new integrations added regularly. Every channel is included in your plan with no per-channel fees, unlike some competitors who charge extra per marketplace.

Can SellerChamp handle the same volume as my current platform?+

Yes. SellerChamp is built for high-volume sellers and handles 10,000+ SKU bulk listings, real-time inventory sync across all channels, automated repricing at scale, and multi-warehouse fulfillment routing. We serve sellers with hundreds of thousands of SKUs across all major marketplaces — volume is never a concern.

What if I need help after I’m set up?+

Our support team is available via live chat, email, and phone — and consistently rated as one of the best in the industry. Unlike enterprise platforms where you wait days for a ticket response, SellerChamp customers regularly comment on how fast and personal the support is. You’ll also get access to our knowledge base and seller community.
