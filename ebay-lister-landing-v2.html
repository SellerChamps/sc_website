Pricing questions

Common questions, answered.

Which plan should I start with?
Pick the tier that fits both your listing count and your monthly order volume. Starter works for under 500 listings and under 250 orders/month. Growth covers up to 2,500 listings and 1,500 orders/month (80% of new customers pick this). Pro handles up to 10,000 listings and 5,000 orders/month. Past that, you’re at Platform scale. Every plan has a 14-day free trial — upgrade or downgrade anytime.
What counts toward my plan limits?
Active listings: a single product live on a single channel. The same product on eBay, Amazon, and Walmart counts as 3. Ended, sold-out, and draft listings don’t count. Orders/month: each unique customer order across all channels, on a rolling 30-day basis — one order with 10 line items counts as 1. We notify you before you hit either limit; upgrade or buy an add-on.
How do channels work? Can I pick which ones?
A channel is a marketplace — eBay, Amazon, Walmart, Shopify, Etsy, TikTok Shop, Mercari, Poshmark, and 6 more (14+ total). Your tier sets how many you activate at once: Starter = 1, Growth = 3, Pro = 5, Platform = custom. You pick any combination and can swap channels anytime from your dashboard.
What’s the difference between a channel and a store?
A channel is the marketplace (eBay). A store is a specific seller account on it. Two eBay accounts = 1 channel, 2 stores. Most sellers have one store per channel; extra stores are +$15/mo each.
How is this different from your enterprise platform?
Starter, Growth, and Pro are self-serve SellerChamp Lister — sign up and you’re live in minutes. SellerChamp Platform is for larger sellers needing PIM, multi-warehouse inventory, 3PL, POS, ERP and accounting integrations, and dedicated onboarding.
What does free migration support include?
Sign up for any plan, then book a migration call with our team. We connect to your current listing tool, import your listings, templates, photos, and item specifics, and verify everything is live on your channels. Most migrations finish in under 24 hours — included free on every plan.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your dashboard. Upgrades are prorated; downgrades take effect at the end of the billing cycle. On annual billing, mid-cycle downgrades are refunded as account credit.
Are there setup fees or hidden costs?
No setup fees, no GMV fees, no per-channel charges, no per-API-call fees. The only optional charges are published add-ons and Platform custom integrations.
What happens after my 14-day trial ends?
If you keep going, your subscription starts on day 15 at the plan you chose. We email you a few days before. Cancel before day 14 and you’re never charged — we ask for explicit confirmation before billing.
